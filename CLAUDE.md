# Gig Lead Responder — Project Instructions

## Three Questions (Mandatory)

Every **brainstorm** and **plan** session MUST end with these three questions
answered in the output document before stopping:

1. **Hardest decision in this session?**
2. **What did you reject, and why?**
3. **Least confident about going into the next phase?**

These are non-optional. Append them as a `## Three Questions` section at the
bottom of the brainstorm or plan document. Do not skip them even if the session
felt straightforward — the "obvious" sessions are where unexamined assumptions
hide.

The work phase already captures these per chunk in HANDOFF.md. The fix-batched
skill has its own hardcoded version. This rule covers the two phases that
previously lacked them.
