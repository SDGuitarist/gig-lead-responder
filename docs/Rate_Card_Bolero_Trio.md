## Product Overview

Traditional Mexican romantic trio featuring:
- Classical guitar
- Requinto guitar (lead)
- Vocals

Authentic bolero repertoire passed down through generations. One of the rarest ensembles in San Diego.

**Best for:** Romantic occasions, anniversaries, upscale Mexican restaurants, fine dining, wine pairing events, cultural celebrations

**Lead time:** 4-6 weeks; Valentine's/holidays 8+ weeks

## Tier Definitions (Quick Reference)

**Tier 1 — Relationship/Investment:** Reserved for prospects with recurring revenue potential or strategic venue access.

**Tier 2 — Standard:** Private parties, social events, one-off celebrations, standard occasions. Price-conscious, comparison shoppers. **90%+ of Bolero Trio clients are T2.**

**Tier 3 — Premium:** Named luxury venues, corporate at upscale properties, milestone celebrations with stealth premium signals. Evaluating fit, not just price.

**Stealth Premium Signals (any ONE = T3):** Premium venue, 150+ guests, La Jolla/Rancho Santa Fe/Coronado/Del Mar/Carmel Valley zip, corporate 100+, buried luxury cues (valet, black tie, plated dinner, VIP, executive audience), Saturday evening at named venue.

## Booking Guidelines

**Anchor:** The number you quote first (left number)  
**Floor:** The number you hold (right number)

**Below floor:** Only if booking has Tier 1 strategic value. Never for one-time client.

**Travel modifiers:** Outside SD County +$250–300, LA County +$500–800

**Holiday/peak dates:** Valentine's, Cinco de Mayo, NYE quoted separately above standard rates

## Strategic Notes

**Scarcity positioning:** Bolero trios are one of the rarest ensembles in San Diego. Authentic requinto playing and traditional repertoire are extremely difficult to source.

**Primary buyers:** T2 clients with cultural connection to bolero tradition. They know it's scarce and coveted, but budget-conscious.

**Negotiation:** Most T2 clients need emotional/scarcity framing to justify investment. See Bolero_Trio_Negotiation_Playbook for positioning language and negotiation sequence.

**Duration adjustment:** 1.5-hour option is primary negotiation lever when 2 hours feels like budget stretch.
