## Buyer Tiers (B2C)

- **Tier 1 — Relationship/Investment:** Flat rate ($500 minimum). Reserved for prospects with recurring revenue potential or strategic venue access. The test: will this booking generate future revenue? **Requires documented strategic value justification.**
- **Tier 2 — Standard:** Private parties, social events, one-off celebrations, standard weddings. Price-conscious, comparison shoppers.
- **Tier 3 — Premium:** Named luxury venues, corporate at upscale properties, rehearsal dinners, milestone celebrations with stealth premium signals. Evaluating fit, not just price.
- **Tier 4 — Duo/Ensemble:** Product tier, not buyer tier. Clients requesting duo/ensemble signal budget capacity.

**Stealth Premium Signals** — any ONE present = Tier 3: premium venue, 150+ guests, La Jolla / Rancho Santa Fe / Coronado / Del Mar / Carmel Valley zip, corporate 100+, buried luxury cues (valet, black tie, plated dinner, VIP, executive audience), Saturday evening at named venue.

**Lead Source Logic** — Direct leads (alexguillenmusic.com) close 80%+ with near-zero competition; anchor high. Platform leads (The Bash, GigSalad) face artificial competition; quote toward platform column regardless of budget.

---

## B2C Negotiation Rules

- **Anchor:** The number you quote first (left number). **Floor:** The number you hold (right number).
- **$500 booking floor is absolute.** No booking under $500 regardless of tier, duration, or configuration.
- Below floor only if the booking has Tier 1 strategic value. Never for a one-time client.
- Travel modifiers: outside SD County (duo/solo) +$250-300, LA County +$500-800.

## Strategic Margin Notes — Solo & Duo Only

At T3 Direct 2hrs — the highest-value B2C booking profile:

**Solo Guitar:**
- **100% margin** — every dollar is yours
- **Best for:** High-volume bookings, quick yes decisions, relationship development
- **T3D 2hrs nets $895** — solid absolute dollar return for zero payout

**Standard Duo:**
- **T3D 2hrs nets $620** after 60/40 split ($1,700 quote - $400 musician cost = $1,300 x 60%)
- **$310/hour net** — strong absolute dollar return
- **Best for:** Clients wanting fuller sound without specialty cultural positioning

**Flamenco Duo:**
- **T3D 2hrs nets $669.50** after 60/40 split ($1,895 quote - $400 cost = $1,495 x 60%)
- **$334.75/hour net** — highest net-per-hour in the duo category
- **Cultural premium justified** — specialty positioning commands higher anchor
- **Best for:** Venues/clients with Spanish/Mediterranean identity, cultural alignment value

**Key Insight:** Flamenco Duo outperforms standard duo by $49.50 per 2-hour service at T3D. When cultural fit exists, always position flamenco duo as the recommendation.

**B2B Weekly Residency Comparison (2hrs):**
- Solo: $500 (100% margin = $500 net)
- Standard Duo: $850 ($850 - $400 cost = $450 net)
- Flamenco Duo: $900 ($900 - $400 cost = $500 net, same as solo)

**Takeaway:** With the $500 floor, solo weekly residency now matches flamenco duo net margin. Flamenco duo still wins on cultural differentiation and client perception of premium value.
